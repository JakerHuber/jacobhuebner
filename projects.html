HuebDesk

In 2017, I took the Capstone for the Computer and Internetworking Technologies Major. The Capstone required students to use all of the skills they learned over the program. The Capstone was to create a fully functional networking with the same functionality of a real business network. For the whole semester, I worked on a team with two other students to build a network. At the end, we had a fully wired cisco network, VMware server running the VMs, windows server for Active Directory, a fully modeled ADDS based off our company, Internet Access, VPN Tunnel, and a webserver.
